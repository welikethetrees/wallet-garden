wallet.garden is your personal garden in the metaverse.

Each Ethereum user can freely generate a Wallet Garden, which is a showcase of your Ethereum address in the metaverse.

Your assets, wallet history and quirks will be displayed, creating a visual representation of your ENS, NFT collection, or activity in Ethereum history, all fully carbon-negative!

How does Wallet Garden work?

Wallet Garden is a completely new, free way to display and visualize your NFTs, assets, wallet history, and participation in the crypto space.
‍
Centered around your central tree trunk (in itself an NFT), which dynamically grows according to your gas usage, you’ll be able to seed & create new floating islands depicting your favorite NFTs from the biggest projects, such as CryptoPunks, Bored Apes, Mooncats. More collections will be added every other week, including limited-editions by your favorite NFT artists.

You'll also get your own private link (e.g. wallet.garden/path.eth) to show your friends, and connect it with other links to your favourite hangouts (Twitter, Discord, websites, etc)!
Why are you building this?

Currently, there is no simple way to showcase your full crypto presence and identity. Trying to explain how long you've been in the space is tiresome (Etherscan history), your NFT collection (OpenSea profile, doesn't have featured function), or your participation in the space (rugged on IDOs? over 10.000 transactions?).

We want to build something that solves that issue, makes it user-friendly, and through its features (fully carbon-negative, diverse team, etc) will be appealing to non-crypto natives as well!

With it being free to mint, your crypto-newbie friends will also be able to start their adventure in the space without feeling guilty about it.
How do I interact with Wallet Garden?

You’ll be able to interact through a web app we’ll be building, in which you’ll be able to control all the interesting features that Eden brings. Actions you take in the web app will dynamically update your Garden NFT.
What will Wallet Garden be built on?

Due to the interactive nature of the project and the high amount of gas transactions that it’ll incur, we’ll be building it on an L2 solution instead of Ethereum.
What are some of the key features of Wallet Garden?

• Your central trunk gets bigger according to how much gas you've spent (so, how much you've participated in the space).
• You get badges for key stats (e.g. 1.000 transactions made, 3-year old wallet, holds 5 ETH, etc) and participation in certain events (e.g. ETH launch, staked ETH, held during Byzantium fork).
• You can roll for random traits as many times as you want (e.g. looking for a Weeping Willow trunk with frozen island and a dog? Just keep rolling (it's free!) until you hit it, then mint once you do!)
• Passively offset carbon just by holding a garden! You'll have a small counter at the bottom of your screen that slowly goes up, and every milestone you hit, we'll donate to conservation projects in your name!
• Play various mini-games (e.g. fishing!) with your custom characters, interact with other wallets, and more to come!
• Link your socials or other websites to your garden, so you can use it as a homepage for your crypto identity!
How do CryptoTrunks work within Eden?

If you own one or more CryptoTrunks in your wallet, you’ll be able to copy your traits into your new Wallet Garden trunk, so you can have a 1:1 representation of your original NFT!

This way, CryptoTrunk generative elements and Genesis traits will be completely unique to CryptoTrunk holders, and will be unobtainable in new, fresh Wallet Garden trunks — those will have a completely new set of elements and traits.

If you hold multiple CryptoTrunks, you’ll be able to swap between them, and the rest will show up as a forest behind your main one!
How does Wallet Garden look like?

Very similar to this website! Click on 'See Examples' button below to go through a few models of a future garden. We'll be building an entire UI around it together with our beta launch, so it'll become even more customizable.

‍We’ve released a few sneak-peeks of Wallet Garden over on our Twitter account, and we’ll be posting regular art updates as we go along, so be sure to check our feed!
Which NFT projects will you feature?

We’ve taken a look at what are the most popular NFT projects our v1 CryptoTrunks users hold, and have started with 12 of them, including BAYC, Art Blocks, ENS, Mooncats, Pudgy Penguins, and more!

Over time, we'll want to include at least 50+ collections, and we'll be releasing new ones every two weeks to keep up with new trending projects.
Which artists are you going to collab with?

We've currently got a talented team of three pixel-artists which we love dearly, but we're going to be expanding that to other prominent artists within the space. We haven't confirmed any collabs just yet, but keep an eye on our Twitter for future reveals!

Note that proceeds from any artist collabs will go directly to the artists themselves!
Is Eden carbon-negative?
‍
Absolutely! We will continue to partner with Offsetra (& potentially others) in order to offset all our mints and operating footprints by donating and supporting reforestation projects in Central America, the Amazon, and more.

In addition, we’ll offer you the option to directly offset your own emissions or donate through Project Eden, with 100% of the proceeds going to Offsetra, which will earn you special badges within the metaverse!
When is beta launching?

We’re hard at work at developing it, and we’ll let you know about exact dates once we get closer to launch. We aim to get a beta-build out before the full release, so you’ll be able to see it progress in real-time!
Are you going to launch with full features?

We're big believers in listening to user feedback, so we'll be gradually introducing new features and then improving them before moving on to the next shiny thing!

Features that we're planning to launch with: CryptoTrunk-to-Wallet-Garden conversions, infinitely rolling new Garden trunks, passively offsetting CO2, and linking your socials and websites to your Garden.
