Progression

Your main island grows dynamically with your wallet

As you operate more within the crypto ecosystem, your gas costs naturally go up—and to simulate that, the tree on your main island grows accordingly in five tiers, eager to absorb the CO2 you've produced.

Customization

Large collections display your NFT’s custom traits

Assets from more well-known collections will have a special set of islands associated with them, where several types of traits—such as colour, hair, hats. and more—unique to your specific asset will have accurate pixel representations.
