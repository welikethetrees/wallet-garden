wallet.garden is your private infinite plot in the metaverse.

Each Ethereum user can generate a wallet garden, which is a representation of your Ethereum address in the metaverse.

Your assets, wallet history and quirks will be displayed in the garden, creating a visual representation of your ENS, NFT collection or activity in Ethereum history.

How does Eden work?
‍
Centered around your central tree trunk, which dynamically grows according to your gas usage, you’ll be able to seed & create new floating islands depicting your favorite NFTs from the biggest projects, such as CryptoPunks, Bored Apes, Mooncats, and more that’ll be added every other week, including limited-editions by your favorite NFT artists.
How do I interact with Eden?

You’ll be able to interact through a web app we’ll be building, in which you’ll be able to control all the interesting features that Eden brings. Actions you take in the web app will dynamically update your Eden NFT.
What will Eden be built on?

Due to the interactive nature of Project Eden and the high amount of gas transactions that it’ll incur, we’ll be building it on an L2 solution instead of Ethereum.

Is Eden carbon-negative?
‍
Absolutely! We will continue to partner with Offsetra (& potentially others) in order to offset all our mints and operating footprints by donating and supporting reforestation projects in Central America, the Amazon, and more.

In addition, we’ll offer you the option to directly offset your own emissions or donate through Project Eden, with 100% of the proceeds going to Offsetra, which will earn you special badges within the metaverse!

How do CryptoTrunks work within Eden?

If you own one or more CryptoTrunks in your wallet, you’ll be able to copy your traits into your new Eden trunk, so you can have a 1:1 representation of your original NFT!

This way, CryptoTrunk generative elements and Genesis traits will be completely unique to CryptoTrunk holders, and will be unobtainable in new, fresh Eden trunks—those will have a completely new set of elements and traits.

If you hold multiple CryptoTrunks, you’ll be able to swap between them, and the rest will show up as a forest behind your main one!
